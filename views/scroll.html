MERS spread in South Korea
Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) was brought to South Korea in May 2015, and has now spread more rapidly than in any other country. The routes of MERS infection has been unpredictable and there was also a conflict between government and citizens about the government restricting access information on the virus outbreak. This project has been developed to show how MERS spread in South Korea and the events happened around it.

You can see news articles about MERS spread in South Korea in chronological order as well as a time-based visualization showing both the locations of hospitals which are infected with MERS and the number of patients that each hospital holds. The routes of viral spread are also displayed on the network graph page.
